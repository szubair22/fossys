# OrgSuite Roadmap

OrgSuite is being developed in stages, with each stage adding new modules and capabilities.

## Stage 1: Governance (Current)

**Focus**: Meeting management and organizational governance

### Included Modules
- **Dashboard** - Overview and quick actions
- **Governance (OrgMeet)** - Meetings, agendas, motions, voting, minutes
- **Membership** - Basic member tracking and contacts
- **Documents** - File storage and organization documents
- **Administration** - Organization settings, roles, permissions

### Key Features
- Meeting scheduling and lifecycle management
- Agenda builder with topics, motions, elections
- Motion workflow (draft > submitted > screening > discussion > voting)
- Electronic voting with quorum tracking
- Video conferencing via Jitsi Meet
- Auto-generated meeting minutes
- Role-based access control (Owner, Admin, Member, Viewer)

---

## Stage 2: Collaboration

**Focus**: Real-time communication and collaboration

### Planned Features
- Chat messaging (organization and meeting channels)
- Real-time notifications
- Presence indicators
- Message threading and reactions
- File sharing in chat

---

## Stage 3: Membership Enhancements

**Focus**: Advanced member directory and management

### Planned Features
- Enhanced member profiles
- Membership tiers and dues tracking
- Member self-service portal
- Bulk member import/export
- Member activity history
- Customizable member fields

---

## Stage 4: Dashboard Metrics

**Focus**: Analytics, reporting, and CRM

### Planned Features
- **Dashboard Metrics**
  - Key performance indicators
  - Meeting attendance trends
  - Membership growth charts
  - Custom metric definitions

- **CRM Module**
  - Lead management
  - Opportunity tracking
  - Activity logging
  - Pipeline visualization

---

## Stage 5: Finance

**Focus**: Financial management and revenue tracking

### Planned Features
- Chart of Accounts (double-entry bookkeeping)
- Journal entries with posting workflow
- Donation tracking and receipts
- **Contract Management**
  - Service contracts with milestones
  - Performance obligations
- **Revenue Recognition (ASC 606-lite)**
  - Deferred revenue schedules
  - Recognition rules engine
  - Revenue waterfall reports

---

## Future Considerations

- Mobile app (React Native)
- API for third-party integrations
- Plugin/extension system
- Multi-language support
- White-label deployment options
- Audit logging and compliance reports
