Everything you need for effective meetings

Streamline your organization's governance with our comprehensive suite of tools.

Agenda Management

Create structured agendas with topics, motions, and elections. Keep your meetings organized and on track.

Motion & Voting

Submit motions, conduct electronic voting with multiple methods, and track results in real-time.

Video Conferencing

Participant Management

Manage roles, voting rights, and attendance. Support for vote delegation and weighted voting.

Speaker Queue

Manage speaking order with points of order support. Track speaking time and ensure fair participation.

Live synchronization across all participants. See votes, speakers, and agenda changes instantly.
